About

AI, data science, and scalable computing for real-world problems.

I am an AI and data science professional with a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of North Texas. My background spans artificial intelligence, machine learning, graph neural networks, high-performance computing, predictive modeling, data visualization, and applied data analytics.

My research and professional work focus on designing intelligent models, scalable algorithms, and data-driven systems for complex datasets in socioeconomic, biomedical, financial, and social-data domains.
